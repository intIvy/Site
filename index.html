The science

A digital twin built from thousands of real bodies.

Behind every recommendation is a body model constructed from thousands of real people's measurements — anonymised, aggregated, and continuously refined. Ella matches you to that model, then maps it against each garment's fabric, cut, and intended fit. Not an algorithm guessing from a brand's outdated chart. Body science, working quietly in the background.

Your data blends into the crowd the moment it's submitted. What Ella learns from you strengthens the model for everyone — and what the model knows makes your recommendation more accurate than anything a size chart can offer.
